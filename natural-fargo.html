Places in/near Fargo to get organic and "natural" foods and health products

  • Sydneys Health Market
    Fresh (local) produce, crackers and chips, dairy and alternatives
  • Tochi Products
    Bulk, specialty foods, herbs and spices; vitamins, health and beauty; home brewing supplies and unique gifts. Tochi Products on Facebook
  • Swanson Health Products
    Vitamins, nutrition, skincare, and food and beverage products. Also available by catalog. (Swanson Health retail store on Facebook)
